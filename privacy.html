Blueprint Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Blueprint provides AI study tools and optional Canvas sync. This policy explains what data is used by the Blueprint Canvas Connector Chrome extension.

What the Extension Does

The extension runs on Canvas pages and, when you click Sync, collects your Canvas academic data (courses, assignments, grades, planner items, calendar events, announcements, modules, submissions, discussion topics, syllabi, and inbox conversations) and sends it to Blueprint.

Data Sent

  • Canvas course and academic data listed above
  • A Blueprint device token used to connect your browser to your Blueprint account
  • Sync timestamps and basic sync status metadata

Data Not Collected

  • The extension does not ask for or collect your Canvas password
  • The extension does not collect data from non-Canvas websites
  • The extension does not sell personal data

Where Data Is Sent

Synced data is transmitted securely over HTTPS to Blueprint services used to process Canvas imports and account-linked data.

Storage

The extension stores only connector settings in Chrome extension storage (pairing token, UI preferences, and sync metadata). Your synced academic data is stored in Blueprint backend infrastructure.
